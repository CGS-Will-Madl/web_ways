HTML Lesson 5 - Validation

Validation is where a program runs through your code making sure it is all perfect and readable by every browser, it detects any issues and tells you where it is and what is wrong with it. Although most of the errors wont actually affect the outcome of the code it just makes it all proper and correct and perfect.

To validate a website that is XHTML (HTML strict) you must put this at the very top of your code:

<!DOCTYPE html PUBLIC "-//W3C//DTD XHTML 1.0 Strict//EN" "http://www.w3.org/TR/xhtml1/DTD/xhtml1-strict.dtd">
